<h1 style="background-color:thistle;"> Digital Audio</h1>


<p>For my <b>Digital Audio Mini Project 3</b>, I chose to create an introduction to use for my ePortfolio page with the <b>Audacity</b> software.</p>
<p>I have a background in radio and television production which influenced how I created my Mini Project 3. I opened with a sound clip, faded the first clip down, began my voice over, faded the first clip out, faded the second clip up, under my voice over and ended with a quick fade out at the end.</p>
<p>I received permission to use track 7 <i>Corporate Technology</i> and track 9 <i>Corporate Vision</i> from  Scott Holmes' <i>Corporate Motivational Music</i> album at <a href="http://scottholmesmusic.com">scottholmesmusic.com</a>. His content is available with a FREE Creative Commons License.</p>
<p>Below the audio clip are the editing effects I utilized in Audacity to develop my project.</p>

<audio controls>
<source src="reed.mp3" type="audio/mp3">
</audio>

<p>To edit the music tracks, I:</p>
	<ul>
		<li> <b>imported</b> the first music file.</li>
		<li> used the <b>time shift tool</b> to add .5 second before the audio begins.</li>
		<li> <b>clipped</b> the music about 20 seconds in and used the <b>envelope tool</b> to fade out the clip at about 17 seconds.</li>
		<li> <b>imported</b> the second music file.</li>
		<li> <b>clipped</b> the beginning of the music to  20 seconds in and used the <b>envelope tool</b> to fade in the clip.</li>
		<li> used the <b>time shift tool</b> to place it at about the 20 second mark.</li>
		<li> used the <b>envelope tool</b> to fade out the clip.</li>
	</ul>

<p>To create the voice over, I:</p>
	<ul>
		<li> used <b>Logitech H390 USB computer headset</b>.</li>
		<li> selected the headset in the <b>recording device</b> dropdown.</li>
		<li> used the <b>time shift tool</b> to add place the voice over near the 10 second mark.</li>
		<li> used the <b>silence audio</b> to remove the clicks at the beginning and end as well as the breath intake between statements.</li>
	</ul>		

<p>To finalize the project, I:</p>
	<ul>
		<li> <b>normalized</b> the audio.</li>
		<li> <b>mix and rendered</b> the tracks.</li>
		<li> <b>exported</b> to a .mp3.</li>
	</ul>
