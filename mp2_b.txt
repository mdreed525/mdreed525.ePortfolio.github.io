<h1 style="background-color:thistle;"> Photoshop Before and After: Photo 1</h1>


<p>For my first <i>before and after</i> assignment, I chose to correct this photo to utlize as my head shot on my ePortfolio website.</p>
<p>The photo was taken with a <b>Nikon D3300</b> in bright sunlight. I feel the corrections were an improvement and created a better version of this photo.</p>
<p>To correct this photo to my liking, I:</p>
	<ul>
		<li> used the <b>clone tool</b> to fill the flowers in behind my right sholder to cover the exposed dirt.</li>
		<li> used the <b>dodge tool</b> to brightened up my eye area to hide my dark circles.</li>
		<li> adjusted 
			<UL>
				<li><b>hue</b>.</li>
				<li> <b>levels</b>.</li>
				<li> <b>vibrance</b>.</li>
				<li> <b>saturation</b>.</li>
				<li> <b>color balance</b>.</li>
	</ul>
	</ul>

<h1 style="background-color:thistle;"> Photoshop Before and After: Photo 2</h1>
